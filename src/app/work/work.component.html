How does the PDM Monitor work?

The PDM Monitor divides the database structure into hierarchies:

Account (The Corporation) .
Plant (The local plant).
Area (Self Describes) .
Route-based Vibration Monitoring Systems .
System (A group of Machines put together by purpose, location or similarity) .





The Analyst walks the plant collecting vibration readings on the machines in the route, one by one.

Once data is collected, the analyst will go and download the data on the desktop computer, ready for analysis and reporting.

The PDM MONITOR uses all the thecnologyes for the best job for you .
